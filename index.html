Abbreviated conversations about this and that and architecture

Text messages are like love notes – brief, sparse and intimate. They reveal different things than spoken conversation – different selves.

We asked some friends who happen to be architects if they'd mind us eavesdropping on their text chat for a week or so and publishing it on a website. They said yes, so we did.

We asked if they had friends who would do the same. They said yes again, so we're going to start posting more chats – maybe one a month or something.

Check in from time to time and eavesdrop on architects chatting by text about... whatever architects chat about.

Or we can email you snippets of chats when we post them.

Or... leave us your number and we'll text you when there's something new on the site. Don't worry, we won't call you. Phone calls aren't really our thing.
